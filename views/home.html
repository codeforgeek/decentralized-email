About D-Mail

Dmail is a peer to peer email system built on top of Maze framework, offering an alternative to the existing email system with better features and security.

Peer-2-Peer

Unlike other email systems, D-Mail is completely decentralized, hence there is no central ownership

Security

D-Mail is end-to-end channel encrypted. Nobody, except you, not even our engineers - can access your data.

Offline First

Download our desktop application and use it without internet, once your're online everything will be synced.

Spam Protection

D-Mail has built-in spam protection. You can send or receive emails only from approved contacts. No more fake lottery emails!
